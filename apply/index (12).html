How to Prepare for an Interview

Research the company that best suits your role. Also prepare for common interview questions in the field you have applied.

You should also dress properly, arrive on time for interview, and bring copies of your resume and any other necessary documents.

  • Research the company and the role
  • Prepare for common interview questions
  • Dress appropriately
  • Bring copies of your resume and other necessary documents
  • Practice your responses
